What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet or mobile phone. They do not pose a threat to your computer and security, and despite false representations, they have nothing in common with viruses such as Trojan Horse. When you visit a website, cookies help the website to "remember" you and your activity on the site for several visits, which means that this site will work faster for you. If you want to know more about cookies, you will find a lot of information about them on the Internet.

If you do not want to receive cookies, you can configure your browser to receive messages when sending cookies, or block them altogether. You can also delete cookies that have already been downloaded.
